About

We are South Doyle's Team Soknorobo.

2017-2018

We are quickly becoming a competitive team in the East Tennessee region. The amount of support we have received has been indescribable. This year, our true hard work and dedication paid off. We were on the winning alliance of the Battle for the Bluegrass 2017 Robotics Competition at Lynn Camp High School, Kentucky. At the Smoky Mountain Regional Competition, as only a second year team, we were ranked 7th overall and alliance team Captain, making it to the Quarter Finals.

2016-2017

The South-Doyle Robotics team competed at the Smoky Mountain Regional Tournament and won big! Our students earned the Rookie All-Star and Highest Seeded Rookie team award out of 48 schools competing. This earned us a qualifying spot at the World Championship in Houston, TX, and a chance to represent South-Doyle on the international stage.
