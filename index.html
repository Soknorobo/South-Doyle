We are Team Soknorobo

We are South Doyle's Team Soknorobo, Winner of the Battle for the Bluegrass 2017 Robotics Competition at Lynn Camp High School in Corbin, Kentucky. Winners of The 'Highest Rookie Seed' award and 'Rookie All Star Award' at the 2017 Smoky Mountain Regional Tournament.

South-Doyle High School

Our school, South-Doyle High School is a Southern Association of Colleges and Schools' accredited high school in Knoxville, Tennessee, operated by the Knox County Schools school district. It's located at 2020 Tipton Station Rd, Knoxville, TN. Our school provides each student access to MacBook Pros, improving productivity and interaction amidst every student. Our school population is unique due to its diversity and includes 60 percent low income students.
